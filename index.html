Intelligent Source-to-Pay Platform

Total Procurement Excellence

A comprehensive platform for every stage of your procurement journey, from strategic sourcing to payment processing.

Purchase Request

Let employees create and submit ad hoc and catalog purchase requests. Connect Amazon Business punch-in and punch-out for faster, simpler, and policy-compliant requests.

Request Review and Approval

Review and validate requests while ensuring policy compliance. Configure hierarchical approvals, custom workflows, automation rules, and tailored fields for complete control.

Strategic Sourcing

Run RFx events, compare vendors, evaluate bids, and shortlist suppliers with Zoho Procurement. Add documents, T&Cs, and assign POCs to the right RFQs for transparency and clarity.

Supplier Management

Enable suppliers to bid, manage POs, submit invoices, and track payments via a self-service portal. Maintain a centralized, auto-synced supplier repository for faster purchase processing.

Contract Management

Get real-time visibility into contract spend. Let auto-extracted data capture key details, track renewals, and monitor contract utilization accurately.

Purchase Order Management

Automatically generate POs from approved requests and RFQs with custom criteria linked to the right supplier. Issue blanket POs for recurring or long-term purchases with ease.

Purchase Receives

Track deliveries, verify items, and record GRNs effortlessly. Log partial receipts for split deliveries or long-running services to ensure accurate matching and payments.

Invoices and Payments

Match invoices with POs and GRNs automatically. With a dedicated invoice inbox, leverage AI-powered invoice extraction and ACH payment options to ensure correct, on-time payments.

Analytics for Smarter Spend

AI-powered advanced analytics uncover hidden savings opportunities, curb maverick spend, and maximize spend under management with greater control and precision.
